## Billing architecture

- Polar checkout and subscription APIs are exposed under `/api/v1/checkout/*` and `/api/v1/subscription/*`.
- Webhook processing for subscription events runs at `/api/v1/webhooks/polar`.
- Plan state is consumed by tenant settings and platform-admin reporting.

## Tenant experience

- Billing UI is available in `/{orgSlug}/settings/billing`.
- Plan status and cancellation workflows are role-restricted (admin-first).
- Checkout success/cancel pages are org-scoped.

## Plan-aware behavior

- Plan enforcement middleware controls selected capabilities and limits.
- Plan status endpoints expose active tier and applicable constraints.
- Billing audit data supports operational traceability.

## Operational recommendation

- Verify webhook signature handling in production.
- Monitor billing retries and failed payment events.
- Alert on plan downgrade/expiry impact for account owners.
