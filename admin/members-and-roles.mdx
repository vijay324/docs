The **Members** page at `/<org-slug>/members` lists everyone in your organization — active members plus anyone with a pending invitation.


## Roles

| Role | Capabilities |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Admin** | Full access. Exactly **one** Admin per organization. |
| **Manager** | Create and manage projects, sprints, and tasks. Approve attendance and leave. Cannot edit billing or change the Admin. |
| **Member** | Work on assigned tasks, log time, request leave, view their analytics. |

The **Single Admin Invariant** means promotions to Admin require demoting the existing Admin in the same step. This is a deliberate guardrail.

## Invite a new member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Invite member">
    Top-right of the Members page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter email and pick role">
    The invitation is unique to the email and valid for 7 days. The role can be Admin, Manager, or Member — pick what fits today.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send">
    Flotick emails the invitee with an accept link. Pending invites show in the Members list until accepted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resend or revoke an invite

Open a pending member and choose:

- **Resend** — regenerates the token and re-emails the invite
- **Revoke** — deletes the pending member entirely

## Promote, demote, or change role

Open a member and pick a new role. Flotick enforces:

- Promoting to Admin requires demoting the current Admin in the same flow
- Demoting yourself to Member requires another Admin to be ready

## Departments and sub-departments

Set a member's **Department** and **Sub-department** to drive analytics and reporting groupings. See [Departments](/admin/departments).

## Soft-delete a member

The **Remove member** action soft-deletes the account. The member appears in [Trash](/features/trash) for 30 days. Their tasks remain assigned (you'll likely want to reassign before removing).

## Permanent delete (Admin)

Admins can permanently delete a soft-deleted member from the Trash before the 30-day window. This is irreversible.

## Seat limits

Each plan has a maximum number of active members. Pending invites also count toward seats. If you hit the cap, upgrade the plan or revoke unused invites.

## Tips

- Audit pending invites monthly. Empty seats save money and reduce noise.
- Use **Manager** generously — it's the role designed for team leads day-to-day.
- Keep the **Admin** role with the founder or operations owner; rotate intentionally if needed.
