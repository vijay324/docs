---
title: "Seat management"
description: "Track which members use plan seats and free capacity safely."
---

An active accepted member uses one organization seat. Pending invitations do not use a seat until accepted.

| Plan | Seat limit |
| --- | ---: |
| Free | 5 |
| Pro | 25 |
| Max | Unlimited |

To free a seat, an Admin can remove a member. Removed members move to Trash and cannot sign in. Their Tasks, comments, history, and other organization records remain.

Restoring a deleted member consumes a seat and can be blocked when the organization is at its limit.

After a downgrade below current usage, Flotick protects the Admin seat and plan-locks the newest excess non-Admin members. Their data is retained. Available capacity or a later upgrade restores the longest-standing locked members first.

## Monitor seats

Review seat usage in **Settings** before sending invitations or restoring deleted members. An invitation can be sent while pending, but acceptance is checked against the current seat limit. If the organization has no capacity, increase the plan or free a seat before the recipient tries again.

## Free a seat responsibly

Open the member record and confirm ownership transfer before removing the person. Reassign active Tasks, review assignments, and testing responsibility when the work must continue. Removal frees the seat only after the member is no longer active; restoring the member later consumes a seat again.

## Plan-locked members

Plan locking is different from an Admin deactivation. A plan-locked member is restricted because capacity fell after a downgrade. When capacity returns, Flotick can restore that member without overriding a separate deliberate deactivation made by an Admin.
