---
title: "Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel"
description: "Manage the organization subscription from billing settings."
---

Only the organization Admin can manage billing.

## Upgrade

1. Open **Settings → Billing and subscription**.
2. Choose a plan and monthly or yearly billing where offered.
3. Review the checkout or upgrade preview.
4. Confirm payment in the hosted checkout.

The billing page updates after Flotick receives confirmation from its billing provider. A mid-cycle paid-plan upgrade can show a preview before confirmation.

## Downgrade or cancel

Use the billing page's available plan-change or cancellation action. Flotick preserves organization data and applies the lower plan through its non-destructive enforcement process.

<Warning>
Before lowering a plan, review member seats, active Projects, storage, Env Storage, Analytics, and custom permissions. Features above the new entitlement become unavailable even though retained data is not deleted.
</Warning>

If a payment or plan status does not update after checkout, refresh billing settings and contact [support](/resources/support) with the organization slug and checkout time.

## Before you upgrade

Check the feature you need and the current organization usage. Pro unlocks several modules rather than only raising a numerical limit; Max adds scheduled reports and removes core usage caps. The billing screen shows the organization plan, status, billing cycle, price when available, and renewal information.

## Before you downgrade or cancel

1. Count active members and active Projects.
2. Review storage and the largest files still in use.
3. Identify work that relies on paid-only modules, workflow configuration, exports, or individual overrides.
4. Decide whether to complete finished Projects, remove members, reduce storage, or upgrade again before the grace window ends.

Flotick retains data during a lower-plan transition. See **Plan changes and retained data** in this section for the resulting access behavior.

## Billing authority

Only an Admin can initiate subscription changes. Managers and Members can view plan information where Flotick exposes it, but cannot modify the organization subscription.
