---
title: "Upgrade or cancel"
description: "How to upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your Flotick subscription."
---

Plan changes are quick, secure, and reversible (where applicable). Billing is handled by [Polar](https://polar.sh), which manages payment, invoicing, and webhook events that keep Flotick in sync with your subscription state.

## Upgrade from Free

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the pricing page">
    Either [flotick.com/pricing](https://flotick.com/pricing) or **Admin → Billing** inside your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a plan and cycle">
    Choose **Pro** or **Max**, and **Monthly** or **Yearly**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pay on the hosted checkout">
    Flotick redirects you to a hosted Polar checkout. Enter card details and confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Land back in the dashboard">
    You return to a confirmation page. Plan limits update within seconds via webhook.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upgrade Pro to Max (mid-cycle)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open billing">
    From **Admin → Billing**, click **Upgrade**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review proration">
    Flotick shows the prorated amount you'll be charged today.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Polar processes the upgrade. Flotick receives the webhook and updates your plan.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downgrade

Downgrades take effect at the **end of the current billing period**. From **Admin → Billing**, pick the lower plan and confirm. You keep current-plan features until period end.

## Cancel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Cancel">
    From **Admin → Billing**, click **Cancel subscription**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Polar marks the subscription to end at the current period's end. You retain access until then.
  </Step>

  <Step title="At period end">
    Your org downgrades to **Free** automatically. Data is retained.
  </Step>
</Steps>

You can re-upgrade at any time before period end to keep your subscription active.

## Failed payments

If a charge fails (expired card, etc.), Flotick:

- Sets your plan status to `past_due`
- Shows a banner with a link to update your payment method
- Retries through Polar's standard dunning schedule

After the grace period, the subscription is canceled and the org moves to Free.

## Refunds

Refunds are handled case-by-case. Email [support@flotick.com](mailto:support@flotick.com) with your org slug and the issue.

## Tips

- Yearly plans aren't refunded on downgrade — you keep features until period end.
- Update your card a couple of weeks before expiry to avoid the dunning queue.
- Save invoices from the billing portal monthly for your finance records.
