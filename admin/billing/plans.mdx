Flotick has three plans. The current pricing and full feature list live at [flotick.com/pricing](https://flotick.com/pricing). This page summarises what each plan unlocks.

[IMAGE: Pricing comparison table with Free, Pro, Max columns]

## Free

For small teams getting started.

- Core productivity: projects, sprints, tasks, personal board
- Attendance, leave, and basic analytics
- Real-time updates
- Basic AI Summaries with monthly limit
- Up to a small fixed number of members and projects

## Pro

For growing teams that need more headroom and AI.

- Everything in Free, plus:
- Higher AI usage limits across summaries and insights
- Advanced analytics views (department slicing, exports)
- Workload balance and bottleneck insights
- Higher member, project, and storage limits
- Priority support response

## Max

For organizations standardizing on Flotick.

- Everything in Pro, plus:
- The highest AI limits
- All advanced analytics including custom date ranges
- The largest member and project limits
- Premium support and onboarding assistance
- Roadmap input

## Currency and billing cycle

Flotick auto-detects your region:

- **United States** — billed in USD
- **India** — billed in INR

Pick **monthly** or **yearly** billing. Yearly billing typically saves 17–20% versus monthly.

## How seats work

Active members and pending invitations each consume one seat. Soft-deleted members do not consume seats. See [Seat management](/admin/billing/seat-management).

## Plan limits enforced in-app

When you approach a plan limit (members, projects, AI usage), Flotick surfaces a banner with the option to upgrade. Some limits are hard caps (e.g., member count); others are soft warnings.

## Change your plan

See [Upgrade or cancel](/admin/billing/upgrade-or-cancel).

## Tips

- Start on **Free** — many small teams ship a quarter or more without paying. Upgrade when AI or seats become the bottleneck.
- Consider **Yearly** billing if you've used Pro for two months and still rely on it.
