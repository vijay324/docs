## Core limits

| Limit | Free | Pro | Max |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| Members | 5 | 25 | Unlimited |
| Active Projects | 3 | 15 | Unlimited |
| Organization storage | 500 MB | 5 GB | 100 GB |
| Maximum file size | 25 MB | 100 MB | 500 MB |
| General AI allowance / month | 100 | 10,000 | Unlimited |
| Orbit Agent allowance / month | 10 | 2,000 | Unlimited |
| Orbit allowance / month | 210 | 22,000 | Unlimited |
| Analytics ranges | 7 and 30 days | Through all time and custom | Through all time and custom |

All plans support Project, Sprint, Task, and unassigned file uploads within their storage and file-size limits.

Pro adds team Analytics and export, saved views, workflow appearance customization, Env Storage, team attendance, advanced leave, model selection, and per-member permission overrides. Max adds delivery forecasting and scheduled reports and removes configured member, active-Project, and AI usage caps.

<Note>
Max storage and per-file size remain capped. "Unlimited" removes the configured product limit for the row where it appears; normal access and fair-use controls still apply.
</Note>

## How limits are applied

Limits are organization-wide unless the product states otherwise. Flotick checks entitlement when you take an action—for example, when accepting an invitation, creating or restoring a Project, uploading a file, or starting an AI request.

Review effective usage and limits in **Settings → Usage & Billing**. An organization-specific entitlement can change the effective allowance shown there.

Review **Plan changes and retained data** in this section before lowering a plan.
