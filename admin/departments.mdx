---
title: "Departments"
description: "Organize members in a two-level department structure."
---

Flotick supports a main department and an optional sub-department for each member. Department choices appear in onboarding, profile management, member administration, and supported analytics filters.

Admins manage the organization department structure from workflow settings. Create or rename a main department, add sub-departments beneath it, and assign members from their profile or member record.

<Note>
A sub-department must belong to the selected main department. Changing a member's main department may require selecting a new sub-department.
</Note>

Department changes affect future grouping and filtering; they do not rewrite historical work ownership.

## Set up departments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open workflow configuration">
    Go to **Settings → Workflow configuration** and open the department management area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the main department">
    Add the top-level group, such as Engineering, Operations, Sales, or People.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add sub-departments where useful">
    Create a second-level group only when it improves routing or reporting, such as Engineering → Platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign members">
    Set each member's department and optional sub-department from their profile or member record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Keep names clear and stable. Renaming a department changes how it is shown in current member records and filters, while historical Tasks and attendance remain attributable to the member who performed them.

<Tip>
Use departments for people structure. Use Projects for delivery work and labels for cross-cutting classification.
</Tip>
