---
title: "Leave policy"
description: "Configure leave types, balance rules, and request validation."
---

Admins manage leave policy from **Settings → Attendance and leave policies**.

Depending on the active plan, controls include:

- Enabled leave types and display names
- Default annual balances
- Half-day support
- Weekend and holiday treatment
- Minimum notice and maximum request length
- Overlapping-request validation
- Accrual cadence and carry-forward limits
- Comp-off expiry
- Encashment eligibility and maximum days

Policy changes affect validation and future balance calculations. Existing approved requests remain recorded.

Use **Reset** to restore Flotick defaults. Review balances after changing accrual, carry-forward, or enabled leave types.

## Configure leave types

Start by enabling only the leave types your organization actually uses. For each type, set the visible label and balance behavior. A clear setup prevents members from selecting an invalid category and keeps reports understandable.

## Configure request rules

Define whether half-day requests are allowed, how weekends and holidays affect the charged day count, the required notice period, maximum request duration, and whether overlapping requests are blocked. These settings are applied when a member submits or an authorized reviewer evaluates a request.

## Configure balance lifecycle

On plans with advanced leave controls, set accrual period, carry-forward limits, comp-off expiry, and encashment rules. Run accrual or carry-forward only after checking the intended dates and balances. Flotick records transactions so the later balance can be explained.

## Review before publishing

Test a representative request after a major policy change: a normal full day, a half day, a holiday overlap, and a balance-limited request. This confirms the member experience matches the policy your organization intended.
