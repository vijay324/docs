---
title: "Member permissions"
description: "Adjust a member's module operations without changing their role."
---

Per-member overrides are available on Pro and Max. Free organizations use role defaults.

Open a member's **Permissions** panel. Supported modules expose eligible `View`, `Create`, `Edit`, and `Delete` operations.

## How access is resolved

1. Admins retain full organization access.
2. The role defines the maximum operations a member can receive.
3. An override enables or disables an operation within that ceiling.
4. An unset operation inherits the role default.
5. Plan entitlement, organization membership, visibility, and Project access still apply.

Supported modules include Inbox, Tasks, Sprints, Projects, My Board, Attendance, Leave, Analytics, Files, Env Storage, and Team Members.

Managers can edit eligible Member permissions but cannot exceed the Member role ceiling or manage Admin access. Use **Reset to role defaults** to remove all overrides.

<Tip>
Use overrides for a specific exception. Use Project membership for Project access and Departments for organization structure.
</Tip>
