---
title: "Attendance policy"
description: "Set organization defaults for working hours and attendance entry rules."
---

Admins manage attendance policy from **Settings → Attendance and leave policies**. Policy customization is available on Pro and Max.

## Available controls

- Standard hours per day
- Overtime threshold in hours
- Default work type: `Office`, `Remote`, or `Hybrid`
- Whether attendance is required on weekends
- Whether members can create backdated entries

Save changes to apply the updated rules to later attendance actions. **Reset** restores Flotick's defaults.

<Note>
Changing the policy does not remove historical attendance records or regularization decisions.
</Note>
