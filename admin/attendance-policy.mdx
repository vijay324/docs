---
title: "Attendance policy"
description: "Set organization defaults for working hours and attendance entry rules."
---

Admins manage attendance policy from **Settings → Attendance and leave policies**.

Policy controls include:

- Standard workday start and end times
- Working days and weekend behavior
- Grace period and late-arrival threshold
- Half-day and full-day hour thresholds
- Overtime threshold
- Whether members may create past-date entries
- The maximum past-date entry window
- Default work type and related attendance rules shown in settings

Save applies the policy to later attendance actions. **Reset** restores Flotick's defaults.

Shift assignments can provide member-specific working hours and thresholds. See [Shifts](/features/shifts).

## Configure the policy

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open policies">
    Go to **Settings → Attendance and leave policies**, then choose attendance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set standard schedule">
    Choose the normal start and end time, working days, weekend behavior, and default work type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set threshold behavior">
    Configure grace, late, half-day, full-day, and overtime thresholds to match your organization rules.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set correction controls">
    Decide whether members can create past-date entries and set the permitted number of past days.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save the policy. New attendance actions use the updated rules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How policy and shifts interact

The attendance policy is the organization baseline. A member's effective Shift can supply its own start, end, working-day, grace, half-day, full-day, and overtime values. Review shift assignments when one member's attendance behavior differs from the organization default.

## Reset safely

Use **Reset** only when you intend to restore Flotick's default policy. Reset changes future validation and calculations; it does not erase historical attendance records or approval decisions.
