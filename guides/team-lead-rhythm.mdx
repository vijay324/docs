This guide is a recommended rhythm for Managers and Admins running a team in Flotick.

## Daily (about 10 minutes)

- Open the team **Attendance** overview to see who's present
- Skim the team's **Personal Board** view via [Team Analytics](/features/team-analytics) to spot stuck work
- Open the **Inbox → AI Insights** tab; act on or dismiss
- Acknowledge `@mentions` and review queues

## Weekly (about 30 minutes)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pulse check on every active sprint">
    For each active sprint:

    - Compare burndown to ideal
    - Read the latest scope events
    - Generate the **AI Sprint summary** for a fast read

    Decide whether to drop scope, rebalance, or stay the course.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Workload review">
    Open the **Workload balance** AI template (or eyeball Team Analytics). Reassign where one or two members are buried.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Leave and attendance compliance">
    Open the **Leave** team tab. Approve pending requests; clarify any irregular attendance with the member.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate the weekly summary">
    Run the **Weekly summary** AI template at org or project scope. Share in your team channel.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Monthly

- Run the **Monthly summary** AI template for stakeholder updates
- Review **Team Analytics** trends — completion rate, attendance regularity, leave volume
- Confirm payroll prerequisites are clean (compensation set, attendance accurate, leave approved) before [running payroll](/guides/running-payroll)

## Quarterly

- Re-evaluate **Workflow policy** — are the WIP limits still right?
- Review **Leave policy** — accruals, balances, encashment limits
- Check **Departments** — do the groupings still match how the team actually works?

## Tips

- Treat **AI Summaries** as your inbox of artifacts; the team appreciates a clean weekly recap.
- Don't let unread notifications pile up. Triage daily, even if briefly.
- The fastest way to spot risk is the burndown plus the AI Sprint summary, side by side.
