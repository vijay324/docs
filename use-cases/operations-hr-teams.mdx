## Typical goals

- Standardize attendance and leave behavior.
- Reduce manual payroll preparation effort.
- Improve compliance and policy adherence.

## Recommended setup

1. Define attendance and leave policies.
2. Train managers on approval workflows.
3. Establish a month-end validation routine.

## Expected outcomes

- Cleaner records.
- Faster approvals.
- More accurate payroll cycles.
