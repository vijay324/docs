---
title: "Growing Organizations"
description: "Scale team execution without losing structure, accountability, or visibility."
---

## Typical goals

- Keep workflows consistent across new teams.
- Prevent reporting fragmentation.
- Maintain clear ownership as complexity grows.

## Recommended setup

1. Standardize workflow templates by team.
2. Use role-based access for governance.
3. Review analytics at team and org level weekly.

## Expected outcomes

- Better cross-team alignment.
- Faster onboarding of new teams.
- Stronger operational discipline.
