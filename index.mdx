## Where to begin

- **New Admin:** follow the [quickstart](/get-started/quickstart), then configure [members and roles](/admin/members-and-roles).
- **Manager:** start with [Projects](/features/projects), [Sprints](/features/sprints), and [Analytics](/features/analytics).
- **Member:** open [Inbox](/features/inbox), review your Tasks, and check in from [Attendance](/features/attendance).

<Note>
Availability depends on plan, role, individual permission, and access to the relevant Project or record.
</Note>

## Documentation map

**Get started** covers access, onboarding, concepts, and navigation. **Work management** covers personal work, Projects, Sprints, collaboration, files, and environments. **Team operations** covers attendance, leave, members, and policies. **Insights and AI** covers Analytics, reports, Orbit, and AI-assisted work. **Admin and account** covers configuration, billing, security, and recovery.
