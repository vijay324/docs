## Where to begin

- **New Admin:** follow the [quickstart](/get-started/quickstart), then configure [members and roles](/admin/members-and-roles).
- **Manager:** start with [Projects](/features/projects), [Sprints](/features/sprints), and [analytics](/features/analytics).
- **Member:** open [Inbox](/features/inbox), review your Tasks, and check in from [Attendance](/features/attendance).

<Note>
Features and limits can vary by plan, role, and individual permission. Flotick hides actions you cannot use and validates access again when you save.
</Note>

## How this documentation is organized

**Get started** explains account access, onboarding, and navigation. **Work management** covers Projects, Sprints, Tasks, collaboration, files, environments, and recovery. **Team operations** covers attendance, leave, holidays, shifts, and administration. **Orbit and analytics** explains intelligence, reports, and measurement. **Admin and account** documents configuration, billing, and personal security.

Each guide identifies the relevant role or plan where it matters and focuses on what is currently exposed in the Flotick application. The documentation does not describe internal operator tools or disabled pre-launch modules.
