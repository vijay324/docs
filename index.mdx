## What you can do in Flotick

- Plan projects and sprints with clear ownership and deadlines.
- Track tasks in list, board, and calendar views.
- Run attendance, leave, and payroll without tool switching.
- Monitor team performance with analytics and AI-powered insights.
- Keep teams aligned with real-time updates and shared visibility.
