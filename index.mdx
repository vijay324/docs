## Built for two audiences

Flotick has clear separation of duty.

- **Members** (Admin, Manager, Member) work inside their organization at `app.flotick.com/<org-slug>/...`.
- **Platform staff** manage Flotick itself through a separate, IP-restricted super admin panel.

Most of these docs apply to organization members. Anything Admin-only is clearly labelled.
