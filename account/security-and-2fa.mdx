Flotick takes account security seriously. This page covers everything you control directly from your profile.

[IMAGE: Profile security section with password, 2FA, and Google link controls]

## Change password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the change-password dialog">
    Profile → Security → **Change password**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter current and new passwords">
    The strength meter must reach acceptable strength. Common and breached passwords are rejected.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save">
    Flotick verifies your current password, hashes the new one with Argon2id, and **revokes every other session**. You stay signed in only on the device you're using.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Two-factor authentication (TOTP)

Two-factor authentication adds a 6-digit code from an authenticator app on top of your password.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable">
    Profile → Security → **Enable 2FA**. Flotick shows a QR code and the secret.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan with your app">
    Use Google Authenticator, 1Password, Authy, or any TOTP app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter the first code">
    The 6-digit code from the app activates 2FA. The plaintext secret is then cleared from Flotick's storage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To **disable** 2FA, you'll be asked to enter a current 6-digit code as confirmation.

## Sign-in flow with 2FA

Once 2FA is on, sign-in adds a step:

1. Identifier and password as usual
2. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator
3. Optionally **trust this device** for future sign-ins

You have 3 attempts within 5 minutes per code request. Failed codes can request a new one.

## Google sign-in

Link or unlink Google from the **Security** section.

- **Link Google** — adds Google as a sign-in option for your account
- **Unlink Google** — removed only if you have a working password set, so your account isn't locked out

## Account lockout

Too many failed sign-in attempts result in temporary lockouts:

- Exponential backoff: 15 minutes → 30 minutes → 60 minutes max

Successful sign-in resets the counter. If you're locked out repeatedly, contact your Admin or [support](/resources/support).

## Tips

- Use a unique password manager-generated password.
- Enable 2FA. It's the single best protection beyond your password.
- Don't share TOTP codes — they're valid for 30 seconds and any sharing means re-issuing.
