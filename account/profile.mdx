Your profile lives at `/<org-slug>/profile`. It's the page to keep current — every interaction with teammates flows through it.


## What you can edit

| Field | Notes |
| :--- | :--- |
| First name and last name | Shown on every card and in mentions. |
| Job title | Shown in member directory and on assignments. |
| Mobile number | For HR and emergency contact (visibility configurable). |
| Profile picture | Up to 5 MB; JPG, PNG, or WebP. |
| Bio | A short introduction shown in member detail. |
| LinkedIn and GitHub URLs | Optional links displayed in member detail. |

## Profile picture

Click the avatar to upload a new image. The image is uploaded directly to secure storage; existing avatars are replaced. Maximum 5 MB.

## Job and department

Job title is free text. **Department** and **Sub-department** are managed by Admins from the Members page or settings.

## Tour again

Click **Tour again** to re-run the onboarding wizard if you want a refresher on the dashboard.

## Privacy

Your profile is visible to other members of your organization. Job title, name, and avatar appear on every comment, task, and member view. If you need a field hidden, contact your Admin or [support](/resources/support).

## Tips

- Keep your name and photo current — they make mentions and reviews far easier to navigate.
- A short, useful bio (one or two lines) helps new teammates know what you do.
