Open `/<org-slug>/profile`. The page is divided into **Personal**, **Security**, and **Connections**.

## Personal details

You can update:

- Username
- First and last name
- Member ID
- Job title and job type
- Main and sub-department
- Mobile number
- Work type: `Office`, `Remote`, or `Hybrid`

Your email and role are displayed with the account. Some organization-managed fields can be constrained by your role or department configuration.

## Security

Email accounts can change their password. Admins and Managers can also manage two-factor authentication from this tab.

## Connections

Connect Google to add Google sign-in to an existing account. You can unlink Google only when another working sign-in method remains, preventing accidental account lockout.

Incomplete required fields contribute to your profile-completion status and may trigger a reminder.

## Update your profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile">
    Select your profile from the organization navigation, then open the **Personal** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the required work details">
    Update your name, username, member ID, title, department, job type, mobile number, and work type as permitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save changes">
    Review the validation messages and save. Flotick refreshes your active profile after a successful update.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your username is used in member URLs and your name is shown on work, comments, and member views. Use a recognizable, professional identity so teammates can correctly assign and mention you.

## Department and work type

Department and sub-department choices come from the organization configuration. If the correct option is missing, ask an Admin to update [Departments](/admin/departments). Work type records whether your normal arrangement is office, remote, or hybrid; it can be used by attendance and reporting surfaces.

## Keep your profile complete

Flotick calculates a profile-completion percentage from required information. If a reminder appears, you can complete the fields immediately, defer it, or dismiss it for the day. A completed profile makes member assignment and people operations more reliable.
