---
title: "First Week Checklist"
description: "A practical checklist to drive adoption in your first seven days."
---

## Day 1-2: Foundation

- Create at least one active project.
- Invite all pilot users and assign correct roles.
- Publish team expectations for task updates and attendance logging.

## Day 3-4: Execution

- Create and assign initial task backlog.
- Move work through board statuses during standups.
- Start attendance and leave workflows for all users.

## Day 5: Visibility

- Review dashboard and analytics with team leads.
- Identify overdue tasks and ownership gaps.
- Adjust sprint scope or priorities where needed.

## Day 6-7: Stabilize

- Validate that all work is tracked in Flotick (no parallel trackers).
- Confirm manager approval loops (leave/payroll prerequisites) are active.
- Capture improvement feedback and apply workflow tweaks.

<Tip>
  Adoption improves most when leaders review Flotick daily and make it the default place for planning and status updates.
</Tip>
