---
title: "Sprint Planning"
description: "A practical framework for planning realistic, high-confidence sprints."
---

## Before planning

- Make sure backlog tasks are refined and scoped.
- Confirm team capacity and availability.
- Identify high-risk dependencies early.

## Planning session

1. Set sprint goal first.
2. Select tasks that directly support the goal.
3. Assign clear owners for each task.
4. Confirm due dates and status expectations.

## During sprint

- Review board progress daily.
- Remove blocked tasks from critical path quickly.
- Protect sprint focus by limiting unplanned work.

## Sprint close

- Compare committed vs completed outcomes.
- Capture lessons and apply them in next cycle.
