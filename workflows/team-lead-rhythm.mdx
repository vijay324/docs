---
title: "Team Lead Rhythm"
description: "Weekly rhythm for team leads to maintain delivery quality and accountability."
---

## Monday: Plan

- Review analytics and overdue work.
- Confirm sprint or weekly priorities.
- Reassign capacity if bottlenecks are visible.

## Mid-week: Unblock

- Check board flow and stalled items.
- Resolve blockers with owners quickly.
- Keep status updates current for leadership visibility.

## Friday: Review

- Review completed work and missed commitments.
- Share concise team summary.
- Capture top process improvements for next week.
