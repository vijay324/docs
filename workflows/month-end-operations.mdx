---
title: "Month-end Operations"
description: "A reliable month-end process for attendance, leave, and payroll readiness."
---

## 1) Validate records

- Check attendance completeness across teams.
- Resolve missing check-outs or inconsistent logs.
- Confirm leave approvals are finalized.

## 2) Prepare payroll inputs

- Review compensation updates.
- Validate payroll structures and exceptions.
- Align with department managers on anomalies.

## 3) Run payroll workflow

- Execute payroll run.
- Review summary for unexpected values.
- Finalize and share payslips.

## 4) Retrospective

- Document recurrent issues.
- Improve policy or workflow settings for next cycle.
