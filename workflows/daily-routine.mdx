---
title: "Daily Routine"
description: "A simple daily operating rhythm that keeps teams aligned and productive."
---

## Morning (10-15 minutes)

1. Check in and set work type in Attendance.
2. Open Dashboard and review top priorities.
3. Update task statuses before standup.

## During the day

1. Execute tasks in priority order.
2. Move tasks across statuses in real time.
3. Flag blockers in comments instead of waiting for meetings.

## End of day

1. Check out with a concise work summary.
2. Update incomplete tasks with next action.
3. Confirm tomorrow's top priorities.
