---
title: "Accept an invitation"
description: "Join an existing Flotick organization from an email invitation."
---

Invitations are issued by an Admin or Manager and expire after the period shown in the invitation email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation link">
    Use the link in your email. Flotick validates that the invitation is pending and has not expired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your account">
    Confirm your details and create a password when requested. The invited email identifies the account you are joining with.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Add your profile details and enter the organization workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If the link is expired or already used, ask the sender to resend the invitation from **Team Members**. Admins and Managers can also cancel pending invitations.

<Note>
An invitation consumes a seat only when accepted. Acceptance is blocked when no seat is available.
</Note>

## What the invitation determines

The invitation connects you to one organization and carries the role chosen by the sender. You can update your profile after joining, but role, organization, and initial access are managed by the organization. If the wrong email received the invitation, ask the sender to cancel it and invite the correct address.

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
| --- | --- |
| Link expired | Ask the sender to resend the invitation. |
| Link already used | Sign in with the account that accepted it. |
| Seat limit reached | Ask an Admin to free a seat or change the plan. |
| Wrong organization or role | Contact the sender before completing onboarding. |

Do not forward an invitation link. It is an account-access link intended for the named recipient.
