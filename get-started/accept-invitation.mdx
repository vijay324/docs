---
title: "Accept an invitation"
description: "Join an existing Flotick organization from an email invite."
---

When an Admin or Manager invites you to their organization, you receive an email from Flotick with a personalized link.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the invitation email">
    The email comes from Flotick and contains a button labelled **Accept invitation**. The link is unique to you and expires after **7 days**. If your link expired, ask the inviter to resend it.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your invitation">
    The accept-invitation page confirms:

    - The **organization name** you're joining
    - The **email** you were invited at
    - The **role** assigned to you (Admin, Manager, or Member)

    If anything looks wrong, contact your inviter before continuing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set your password">
    Pick a strong password. The live meter must reach acceptable strength before you can submit. You can also enter your first name, last name, and mobile number on the same screen.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Once your invitation is accepted, you go through the [onboarding wizard](/get-started/onboarding) to finish your profile and set preferences. After that, you land on your team's dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My invitation link says it's invalid or expired">
    Invitations are valid for 7 days from the time they're sent. Ask your Admin or Manager to open **Members** in their dashboard, find your pending entry, and click **Resend**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The email I was invited at is already used">
    Each email can be tied to only one active Flotick account. If you already have an account in another organization, contact support so we can help merge or transition you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I never received the email">
    Check your spam folder and search for `flotick.com`. If nothing arrives, the inviter can resend. Pending invites also appear in the Members list with the email shown, so you can confirm the address is correct.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
