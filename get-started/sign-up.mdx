Flotick supports two ways to create an account: signing up to start a brand-new organization, or accepting an invitation to join an existing one.

## Start a new organization

Anyone can create an organization. The first user becomes the Admin and is responsible for billing, settings, and inviting their team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the sign-up page">
    Visit [app.flotick.com/auth/signup](https://app.flotick.com/auth/signup).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose an authentication method">
    Pick one:

    - **Email and password** — set a strong password that meets the strength meter
    - **Google** — sign up with a single click using your Google account

    [IMAGE: Sign-up screen with email and Google options]
  </Step>

  <Step title="Name your organization">
    Provide an organization name. Flotick will suggest a URL-safe slug like `acme-co`. You can change the slug if it's already taken or if you prefer something else.

    Your dashboard URL becomes `app.flotick.com/<your-slug>/...` and is visible to your team.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your device (when prompted)">
    If sign-in is from a new device, Flotick may send a 6-digit code to your email. Enter it to verify the device and optionally trust it for future sessions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    You land on the [onboarding wizard](/get-started/onboarding). Fill in your profile and basic organization preferences. You can skip optional steps and return later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Join an existing organization

If your team already uses Flotick, an Admin or Manager will send you an email invitation. See [Accept an invitation](/get-started/accept-invitation).

## Password requirements

Flotick enforces a strong password policy:

- A minimum length and mix of characters indicated by the live strength meter
- Common and breached passwords are rejected
- Passwords are hashed with Argon2id before storage

If you forget your password, use [forgot password](https://app.flotick.com/auth/forgot-password). Resetting your password signs you out of every device.

## Two-factor authentication

You can enable TOTP-based two-factor authentication from your [profile](/account/security-and-2fa). Once enabled, sign-in requires a 6-digit code from your authenticator app in addition to your password.

## Single sign-on with Google

Google sign-in is available wherever email sign-up is. You can later link or unlink a Google account from your profile. Unlinking is blocked if it would leave your account with no way to sign in.

## What happens after sign-up

- A free-plan organization is created
- You become its single **Admin**
- A pending **member** record is created for every email you invite
- You're routed to onboarding, then to your new dashboard
