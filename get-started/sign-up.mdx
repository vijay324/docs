---
title: "Sign up"
description: "Create a Flotick account and a new organization."
---

Use sign-up when you are creating a new organization. If your team already uses Flotick, ask an Admin or Manager for an invitation instead.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start sign-up">
    Open the Flotick sign-up page and choose email or Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    For email sign-up, enter your name, email, username, and a strong password. Google sign-up uses the verified identity returned by Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the organization">
    Enter the organization name and requested organization details. Flotick creates a unique URL slug for the workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Add your work profile and preferences, then open the organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The creator becomes the organization's **Admin**. Member limits depend on the active plan.

<Warning>
Do not use sign-up to join an existing organization. Accounts and email addresses are unique; use the invitation link your organization sent you.
</Warning>

## Choose the right path

Use **email** when you want a password-based Flotick account. Use **Google** when your organization permits Google sign-in and you want Google to verify the account identity. You can later add or remove a Google connection from [Profile](/account/profile), provided another sign-in method remains.

## Organization information

The organization setup asks for information that identifies the workspace, such as organization name and business details. The name appears across the workspace; the generated slug becomes part of signed-in links. Check it carefully before sharing the first Project link with your team.

## If sign-up fails

An email address or username may already belong to an existing account. Try signing in or resetting the password instead of creating a duplicate. If you intended to join an existing organization, request an invitation from its Admin or Manager.
