<Steps>
  <Step title="Create or join an organization">
    [Sign up](/get-started/sign-up), or open an invitation sent by an Admin or Manager.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    Add the requested identity and work details during [onboarding](/get-started/onboarding).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Project">
    Open **Projects**, click **New project**, then set its name, description, visibility, and members.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan a Sprint">
    Create a Sprint in the Project, set its goal, dates, capacity, and members, then add Sprint Tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start work">
    Open **Inbox** for your relevant Tasks. Use **Attendance** to check in and check out.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Press <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>K</kbd> or <kbd>⌘</kbd> + <kbd>K</kbd> to find a page or action.
</Tip>

## First-day checklist

1. Invite a teammate and confirm they can accept the invitation.
2. Add them to the first Project.
3. Create a Sprint Task with an assignee, priority, and due date.
4. Add a comment and attachment.
5. Start the Sprint when its goal, capacity, and commitment are ready.
6. Ask each member to review Inbox and complete their profile.
7. Configure attendance and leave policies before the first team requests.
