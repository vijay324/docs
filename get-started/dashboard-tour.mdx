After sign-in you land on your **Personal Dashboard** at `app.flotick.com/<org-slug>/dashboard`. The left sidebar gives you access to every other area.


## Sidebar navigation

| Section | Where it goes | What you'll find |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Dashboard** | `/dashboard` | Your personal stats, today's attendance, deadlines, and AI summary widget. |
| **Board** | `/board` | A Kanban view of only your assigned tasks. |
| **Tasks** | `/tasks` | All accessible tasks with List, Board, and Calendar views. |
| **Sprints** | `/sprints` | Sprint list with velocity, burndown, and retrospective. |
| **Projects** | `/projects` | All projects you can access. |
| **Attendance** | `/attendance` | Check in or out, view your calendar, and (managers) the team overview. |
| **Leave** | `/leave` | Request leave, see your balance, and (managers) approve requests. |
| **Payroll** | `/payroll` | View your payslips and (admins) manage runs and structures. |
| **Members** | `/members` | Browse teammates and (admins) invite or manage them. |
| **Inbox** | `/inbox` | Notifications and AI insights. |
| **Files** | `/files` | The org-wide file browser with tag filters. |
| **Env Storage** | `/env-storage` | Per-project environment variables and secrets. |
| **Analytics** | `/analytics` | Team and project analytics (managers and admins). |
| **Trash** | `/trash` | Soft-deleted items recoverable for 30 days. |
| **Settings** | `/settings` | Org settings, policies, integrations (admin only). |
| **Profile** | `/profile` | Your personal info, security, and preferences. |
| **Help** | `/help` | In-app help and how-to guides. |

## The personal dashboard

Your dashboard summarizes what matters today.

- **Task counts** by status (Todo, In Progress, Review, Testing, Done)
- **Today's attendance** card with check-in or check-out button and a live work timer
- **Upcoming deadlines** — overdue and at-risk tasks
- **AI summary** widget you can generate on demand for a quick recap


## The top bar

The top bar carries:

- A **search** field that finds tasks, sprints, projects, and members
- A **notifications bell** with the unread count
- The **organization switcher** (if you belong to more than one)
- Your **profile menu** with theme toggle and sign out

## Theme

Flotick supports light and dark themes. Toggle from the profile menu or set your preference during onboarding.
