---
title: "Onboarding"
description: "Complete your work profile before entering the organization."
---

Onboarding runs after organization sign-up or invitation acceptance. The Admin and invited-member flows use the same organization context but present role-appropriate guidance.

## Information collected

- First and last name
- Username and member code
- Job title and job type
- Department and optional sub-department
- Mobile number
- Work type: `Office`, `Remote`, or `Hybrid`
- Theme and product-introduction choices shown in the flow

Your progress is saved to your account. Flotick redirects completed members to the organization Inbox on later sign-ins.

## Profile completion reminders

If required profile fields remain incomplete, Flotick may show an in-app reminder. You can complete the fields, defer the reminder for a few hours, or dismiss it for the current day.

You can update supported fields later from [Profile](/account/profile).

## Complete onboarding well

Use your normal working name, correct job title, department, and work type. These fields help teammates find you in assignment controls and help the organization group attendance and analytics correctly. Enter a mobile number only when it is appropriate for your organization's people operations process.

## Admin and member experience

New organization creators complete the Admin onboarding path and then enter the organization. Invited people complete the Member path after their invitation is accepted. Both paths verify the organization context before sending the member to Inbox, so you do not need to manually construct an organization URL.

## If you need to stop

Your completed information is retained as you progress. Return to the onboarding link or sign in again to continue. If the workspace is unavailable or the organization membership has changed, contact the Admin who invited you.
