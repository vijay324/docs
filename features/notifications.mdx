---
title: "Notifications"
description: "Receive and act on organization events from the in-app notification center."
---

Click the **bell** in the app shell to open unread notifications. The center loads up to 50 unread items and adds new events while you are connected.

Notifications can cover events such as:

- Task assignment, status changes, and comments
- Leave decisions
- Attendance regularization decisions
- Comp-off, reimbursement, and loan decisions when those workflows are active

Click a notification to mark it read and open its linked item when an action URL is available. You can also **Mark all read** or clear the current local list.

The connection indicator shows `Live`, `Connecting`, or `Offline`. Click the offline indicator to retry the real-time connection.

<Note>
Clearing the displayed list does not delete the underlying notification records. Flotick does not currently expose a client-facing channel-preference editor.
</Note>

## Read and act on notifications

Open the bell to see unread items ordered by time. An unread item has an indicator. Click it to mark it as read; when the event has an associated Task or request, Flotick opens the relevant page.

Use **Mark all read** after you have reviewed the current list. The **Clear all** control only clears the local display during that session; it is not a destructive delete of the notification record.

## Connection state

| State | Meaning | What to do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Live** | The real-time connection is active. | No action required. |
| **Connecting** | Flotick is reconnecting. | Wait briefly. |
| **Offline** | No active real-time connection. | Click the indicator to retry, then verify your network. |

New notifications appear while the center is closed and update the unread count. A page refresh retrieves unread items again from the server.
