AI Enhance is a small but loved utility. It takes free-form prose and returns a tidier version — no rewriting your meaning, just better grammar, tighter phrasing, and clearer structure.

## Where to use it

- **Check-out work description** — clean up your end-of-day recap
- **Comment composer** (where supported) — polish a long-form reply before posting


## How it works

Type or paste your text, then click **Enhance with AI**. Flotick sends only the selected text to the AI service, which returns a polished version. The original is never overwritten until you accept the result.

You can:

- **Accept** to replace your text with the polished version
- **Undo** to revert if you don't like it
- **Edit** the polished version before saving

## Tone presets (where available)

Some surfaces let you choose:

- **Concise** — shorter, sharper
- **Formal** — neutral, professional tone
- **Friendly** — warmer, conversational

## Limits

- AI Enhance is rate-limited per user to prevent abuse
- Plan limits apply for very high-volume use
- It only handles plain text and Markdown — not images or files

## Privacy

Only the selected text is sent for enhancement. Sensitive fields and other content stay local to your session.
