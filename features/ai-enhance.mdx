---

AI Enhance is available in supported attendance description fields, including check-out, forgotten-attendance entry, and attendance detail editing.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write a draft">
    Enter at least five characters describing the work completed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enhance">
    Click the Orbit enhance action. Flotick returns a clearer rewritten version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and save">
    Edit the suggestion if needed, then save the attendance record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The enhanced text is not saved until you submit the form. Each request counts toward the organization's AI generation allowance.

## What Enhance changes

AI Enhance rewrites the text you provide into a clearer work description. It is designed to improve readability and structure, not to invent work you did not perform. Review the suggested wording before saving, and correct dates, names, quantities, or technical facts that should remain exact.

## When it is unavailable

The control requires enough source text to work from and an available organization AI allowance. If it fails, keep your original description and try again later. Your typed text remains in the form unless you leave or reset it.

## Good input

Write a short factual draft first: “Investigated checkout timeout, updated retry handling, and verified the staging flow.” This gives Enhance sufficient context. Avoid entering passwords, secret values, private keys, or sensitive personal information in a description you send for enhancement.
