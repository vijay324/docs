---
title: "Scheduled reports"
description: "Email weekly or monthly PDF analytics snapshots to organization recipients."
---

Scheduled reports are a Max-plan feature for Admins and Managers. Open **Settings → Scheduled reports**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose scope">
    Select the full organization or one Project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose cadence">
    Select weekly or monthly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set recipients">
    Enter comma-separated email addresses. Leave the field blank to send to all Admins and Managers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the schedule">
    Flotick calculates the next delivery time and activates the schedule.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After creation, you can send the full report immediately or send a private sample to yourself. Active schedules can be paused, resumed, or deleted.

## Manage an existing schedule

The **Active schedules** list shows the scope, cadence, recipients, state, and next delivery date. Use the switch to pause a schedule temporarily; pausing does not delete its configuration. Delete it when the report should no longer be delivered.

You can update cadence and recipient emails from the schedule controls. The scope is chosen when creating the schedule, so create a separate schedule when you need a different Project or organization-wide audience.

## Delivery behavior

With no recipients listed, Flotick sends the report to the organization Admins and Managers. A **Send report now** action sends the configured report to its recipients. A **Send sample to me** action is safer for checking the format and content before a broad delivery.

<Tip>
Use one organization-wide leadership report and separate Project reports for delivery owners. Keep the recipient list intentional to avoid duplicating routine email.
</Tip>
