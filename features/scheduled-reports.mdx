---
title: "Scheduled reports"
description: "Email weekly or monthly analytics reports to selected recipients."
---

Scheduled reports are available on Max to Admins and Managers.

## Schedule from Settings

Open **Settings → Scheduled reports** to create a PDF snapshot for the organization or one Project.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose scope and cadence">
    Select organization-wide or a Project, then choose weekly or monthly delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set recipients">
    Enter comma-separated addresses, or leave the field blank to include all Admins and Managers.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create and verify">
    Create the schedule, then use **Send sample to me** or **Send report now**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The schedules list shows scope, cadence, recipients, state, and next run. Pause, resume, or delete a schedule from the list.

## Schedule a saved Analytics view

In **Analytics**, select a saved view and click **Schedule**. Choose weekly or monthly delivery, PDF or XLSX, and organization-member recipients. The report preserves the saved scope and filters and includes generated insights.

<Note>
Use an organization report for leadership coverage and Project reports for focused delivery reviews. Check recipients before sending operational or people data.
</Note>
