Flotick exposes AI features through dedicated backend endpoints and product workflows.

## AI capabilities

- Generate tasks (`POST /api/v1/ai/generate-task`)
- Generate sprint artifacts (`POST /api/v1/ai/generate-sprint`)
- Generic generation (`POST /api/v1/ai/generate`)
- Team summaries (`POST /api/v1/ai/team-summary`)
- Template discovery (`GET /api/v1/ai/templates`)
- Summary history and feedback loop endpoints

## In-product AI surfaces

- `/{orgSlug}/dashboard/ai-insights` for admin/manager leadership views.
- AI-powered generation experiences integrated into planning flows.
- Attendance description enhancement in attendance operations.

## Operational guidance

- Keep AI endpoints behind authenticated routes and role checks.
- Store feedback to improve template selection and output quality.
- Publish internal prompting standards for consistency across teams.

<Warning>
  Treat AI output as decision support. Final approval for customer-impacting and compensation-impacting actions should remain with authorized humans.
</Warning>
