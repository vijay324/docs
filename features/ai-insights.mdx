While [AI Summaries](/features/ai-summaries) are on-demand, AI Insights are **proactive**. Flotick continuously analyzes your work data and surfaces notable conditions you should look at — sprints trending off-track, recurring bottlenecks, deadline risk, workload imbalance.


## Where to find insights

Open your [Inbox](/features/inbox) and switch to the **AI Insights** tab.

## What kinds of insights you'll see

- **Sprint at risk** — pace below ideal burndown with significant scope remaining
- **Deadline risk** — multiple tasks approaching due dates without progress
- **Bottleneck detection** — work piling up at a particular workflow stage (often Review or Testing)
- **Workload imbalance** — one or two members carrying disproportionate load
- **Workflow drift** — tasks skipping required transitions repeatedly

## Each insight is actionable

Every insight card has:

- **Title** and short context
- **Suggested action** with a one-click button (e.g., "Reassign", "Notify owner", "Open task")
- **Dismiss** to remove the card with a cooldown before it can re-surface


## How insights are generated

Two trigger modes:

- **Scheduled** — daily background jobs evaluate your org for the enabled insight types
- **Event-driven** — a sudden spike in blocked tasks, an unusual scope event, or other signals can trigger an immediate insight

High-priority insights also send a notification to Admins and Managers.

## Plan availability

Some insight types are available on Pro and Max plans. See [Plans](/admin/billing/plans).

## Privacy

Insights are derived from your org's data only. Flotick respects access rules — an insight referencing a private project is only visible to its members and Admins.

## Tips

- Treat insights as conversation starters with leads, not as automated decisions.
- If an insight fires repeatedly with no action needed, dismiss it; the cooldown will keep it off your screen.
- Pair Insights with the **AI workflow analytics** views to dig into the underlying signals.
