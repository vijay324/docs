## What it does

AI features help teams create better task/sprint drafts and generate concise progress summaries.

## Why it matters

- Cuts planning overhead.
- Improves status clarity for managers.
- Helps teams spot risks sooner.

## How to use it effectively

1. Generate first drafts, then edit for clarity and scope.
2. Use AI summaries for leadership updates.
3. Validate priorities and due dates before publishing work.

## Best practice

Treat AI output as a starting point, not a final decision. Human review keeps quality high.
