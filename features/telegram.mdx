Connect your Telegram account to receive selected Flotick notifications — task assignments, leave decisions, mentions, and daily stand-up prompts — directly in Telegram.

[IMAGE: Settings → Integrations → Telegram tile with Connect button]

## Connect Telegram

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Integrations">
    From the sidebar, click **Settings**, then the **Integrations** tab. Find the Telegram tile and click **Connect**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive a one-time code">
    Flotick generates a code and shows a deep link to the bot, like `https://t.me/FlotickBot?start=link`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the bot and send the code">
    Tap the deep link (or open the bot manually) and send the displayed code. The bot confirms back with a success message.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick the notifications to receive">
    Open **Telegram preferences** to opt selected notification types into Telegram delivery. Only types you turn on are sent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The link code is single-use and expires after about 10 minutes. If it expires, restart from step 1.

## What you'll receive

Common types you can opt into:

- Task assigned, status changed, mentioned in a comment
- Leave request approved or rejected
- Sprint started or completed
- High-priority AI insights
- Daily stand-up prompts (where enabled)

The exact list mirrors what's available in your [notification preferences](/account/notification-preferences). Telegram delivery is on top of in-app notifications, never a replacement.

## Reply to stand-up prompts

If your organization sends Telegram stand-up prompts, you can reply directly in chat. Your reply is captured and posted as a stand-up note in your dashboard.

## Disconnect

Open the integration tile and click **Disconnect**. The bot stops sending you messages immediately, and your Telegram chat ID is removed from your profile.

## What if I block the bot?

If the bot is blocked, Flotick automatically soft-disables your Telegram delivery so it doesn't keep retrying. Reconnect from settings to re-enable.

## Privacy

Telegram delivery is opt-in and per-type. Flotick only sends to the chat you explicitly linked. No data goes to any third party other than Telegram itself.
