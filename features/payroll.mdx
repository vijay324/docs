## What it does

Payroll helps managers and admins organize salary structures, compensation, and payout workflows.

## Why it matters

- Reduces manual reconciliation effort.
- Improves payroll consistency.
- Connects attendance and leave context to compensation operations.

## How to use it effectively

1. Maintain up-to-date salary structures.
2. Verify attendance and leave data before payroll runs.
3. Review payroll run summaries for anomalies.
4. Share payslips promptly after finalization.

<Note>
  Payroll access is role-based to protect sensitive compensation data.
</Note>
