## Comments

Tasks and Sprints support plain-text comments. You can:

- Reply in a thread
- Like or unlike a comment
- Edit your own comment
- Delete a comment when your role permits it

Comments show their author, time, edited state, replies, and likes. Updates appear across connected sessions.

Authors can edit their own comments. Authors, Admins, and Managers can delete comments when they can access the work item.

## Subtasks

Open a Task's **Subtasks** section to add an item with a title and optional description. Mark it complete, reopen it, or delete it. The Task shows completed count and checklist progress.

Completing every subtask does not move the parent Task to `Done`.

<Tip>
Use a subtask for a small checklist item. Create a separate Task when the work needs its own assignee, due date, review, estimate, or discussion.
</Tip>
