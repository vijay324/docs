Inbox is the default page after sign-in. It shows your active work instead of the organization's full Task list.

It includes Sprint Tasks where you participate as assignee, reviewer, tester, or reporter, plus individual Tasks you created. Tasks from completed Sprints are hidden unless you enable **Show completed sprint tasks**.

## Views and filters

- **List:** select a Task and work from its detail pane.
- **Board:** group the same Tasks by status and move them with drag and drop.
- **Calendar:** place Tasks by due date.
- **Filters:** narrow by search, status, priority, type, Project, Sprint, responsibility, label, or date.

Available scopes include **All**, **Assigned**, **Created**, and **Team**, depending on your permissions.

## Work from Inbox

Create a Task, open its full page, or update accessible responsibility, priority, status, dates, description, subtasks, files, comments, and work logs from the detail experience.

<Note>
The header bell is the notification center. Inbox is your personal Task workspace, not a notification history.
</Note>

If a Task is missing, clear filters, confirm its Project, and check whether it belongs to a completed Sprint or [Trash](/features/trash). Your role, permissions, visibility, and Project access determine whether you can view or edit it.

Use the Orbit launcher or open `/<org-slug>/inbox/orbit` for [Orbit Chat](/features/orbit).
