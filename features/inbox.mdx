The Inbox at `/<org-slug>/inbox` is where every notification and AI insight lands. It's the page to open first thing in the morning.


## The two-pane layout

- **Left** — the list of notifications, with filters and bulk actions
- **Right** — the detail of the selected notification, including a quick action button (e.g., **Open task**, **Reassign**)

Click any row to load the detail. Selecting a row auto-marks it as read after a short dwell.

## Filter and find

Filter the list by:

- **Type** — task, sprint, comment, leave, payroll, AI insight, etc.
- **Priority** — low, normal, high, urgent
- **Read state** — unread only, read only, or all
- **Date range**

Search across notification text and entity titles for quick lookup.

## Bulk actions

Multi-select rows to:

- **Mark as read** in bulk
- **Delete** notifications you don't want to keep
- **Mark all read** clears the bell

## AI Insights tab

The **AI Insights** sub-page proactively surfaces what AI has spotted in your organization:

- Sprints trending off-track
- Members likely overloaded
- Tasks with deadline risk
- Workflow bottlenecks (e.g., review queues piling up)


Each insight is a card with:

- A clear **title** and short **explanation**
- A suggested **action** (e.g., "Reassign", "Notify owner")
- **Dismiss** to remove the card with a cooldown before re-evaluation

Insights respect your plan. Some advanced types are available on Pro and Max only.

## Notification preferences

Tune what shows up in your Inbox from **Profile → Notification preferences**. The Inbox respects per-type settings — types you've disabled don't appear here either.

## Tips

- Treat the Inbox like a triage queue: read, decide, act, archive.
- Use AI Insights as a daily nudge — it catches things easy to miss in raw data.
- Pair the Inbox with [Notifications](/features/notifications) preferences to control noise without losing the signal.
