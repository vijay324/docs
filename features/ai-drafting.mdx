---
title: "AI-assisted drafting"
description: "Turn a short brief into editable Project, Sprint, or Task content."
---

Use **Draft with AI** while creating a Project, Sprint, or Task. Flotick returns a structured suggestion without saving or publishing it automatically.

## Where drafting is available

| Surface | Suggested content |
| --- | --- |
| Project | Name and description |
| Sprint | Title, goal, objectives, and success criteria |
| Task | Title, description, acceptance criteria, and planning detail |

Enter a specific brief, generate the draft, then review the preview. You can accept it, reject it, or regenerate before continuing with the form.

<Note>
AI drafting uses the organization's AI enhancement allowance. Availability depends on the active plan and remaining usage.
</Note>

## Write a useful brief

Include the intended outcome, relevant Project or Sprint context, constraints, and definition of success. Verify dates, responsibility, estimates, and acceptance criteria before saving the record.

AI suggestions remain editable. Your normal role, Project access, workflow, and plan limits still apply when you save.
