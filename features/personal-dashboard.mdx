The Personal Dashboard at `/<org-slug>/dashboard` is the page you land on after sign-in. It's tuned to give you everything you need to start the day in one glance.


## What's on the page

| Card | What it shows |
| :--- | :--- |
| **My tasks** | Counts by status: Todo, In Progress, Review, Testing, Done. |
| **Today's attendance** | Check-in / check-out button and live work timer. |
| **Upcoming deadlines** | Overdue and at-risk tasks pulled from your assignments. |
| **Recent activity** | The latest comments, mentions, and notifications. |
| **AI summary** | One-click generation of a quick personal recap. |

## Quick actions

The dashboard exposes the most common actions inline:

- **Check in** or **Check out** for the day
- **Open my Kanban** — jumps to the [Personal Board](/features/personal-board)

## How fresh is the data

Personal dashboard data is cached for short windows for performance, but every cache is invalidated by the events that matter:

- Completing a task, adding a comment, checking in, etc., refresh the relevant cards
- Real-time updates push live counter changes without a refresh

## Customization

Light and dark theme toggles are honored. Cards are responsive and re-flow on mobile widths.

## Tips

- Make the dashboard your start page — bookmark `/<your-org>/dashboard` as the home of your work browser.
- The AI summary widget is fast and free of context-switching — generate it before stand-ups.
- The deadlines card is the cheapest signal in the app for what to start next.
