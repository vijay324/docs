---

## What it does

Tasks capture all actionable work with assignees, priorities, due dates, and progress states.

## Why it matters

- Eliminates ambiguity around who is doing what.
- Increases accountability and execution speed.
- Makes workload balancing easier for managers.

## How to use it effectively

1. Write task titles as clear outcomes.
2. Assign one accountable owner.
3. Add due date and priority before moving to active work.
4. Update status daily so board and dashboard stay trustworthy.
5. Add comments and attachments to keep context centralized.

## Common mistakes to avoid

- Multiple owners for one task.
- Vague task names without deliverables.
- Leaving tasks in stale statuses.
