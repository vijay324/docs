---
title: "My Board"
description: "A Project-scoped Kanban board for work assigned to you or created by you."
---

My Board lives at `/<org-slug>/board`. Select a Project before opening it.

The board includes:

- Tasks assigned to you
- Unassigned Tasks you reported
- Filters for today, tomorrow, the next seven days, and overdue work
- Real-time Task updates

Drag a card to an allowed status or open it for full detail. You can also create a Task in the selected Project.

My Board is intentionally personal even for Admins and Managers. Use the main [Tasks](/features/tasks) page with **Team** scope when you need broader visibility.

## Use the board

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a Project">
    Choose a Project from the sidebar selector or open My Board with a `project` query parameter. The board needs a single Project because its columns follow that Project's workflow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a time filter">
    Use **Today**, **Tomorrow**, **Next 7 days**, or **Overdue** to narrow the personal cards by due date. Select **All** to return to the full personal set.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move or open a card">
    Drag the card to an allowed status, or open it to edit Task details. Workflow validation still applies to a drag-and-drop move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Cards update when other members change the underlying Task. A Task that is reassigned away from you, or an unassigned Task that you no longer report, disappears from this Board after the next refresh.

<Tip>
Use My Board for execution, not reporting. It excludes most work owned by other people by design.
</Tip>
