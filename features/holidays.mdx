---
title: "Holidays"
description: "Maintain the organization's working-day calendar."
---

Authorized Admins and Managers can create, edit, bulk import, and delete holidays.

Each holiday includes:

- Name and date
- Type: national, regional, company, optional, or restricted
- Optional description
- **Recurring** to repeat annually
- **Optional** to identify an optional holiday

The holiday list can be filtered by year and type. Bulk import is available for adding multiple calendar entries at once.

Holidays are used by attendance and leave calculations. A holiday can prevent a day from being treated as a normal working day, subject to organization policy.

## Add a holiday

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Holidays">
    Choose **New holiday** from the Holidays page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the calendar details">
    Provide a name and date, select the holiday type, and add optional notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose recurrence">
    Enable **Recurring** for an annual date and **Optional** when the day is not automatically observed by every member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    The holiday appears immediately in the organization calendar and becomes available to relevant attendance and leave rules.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import a calendar

Use bulk import when setting up a regional or company calendar. Review the file against the required import format before submitting it, then inspect the resulting list for duplicate dates or names. A holiday is unique within an organization by its date and name.

## Maintain the calendar

Edit a holiday when its name, type, or recurrence changes. Delete an incorrect entry rather than leaving an unused calendar record. Before changing an existing holiday, check whether it overlaps approved leave or historical attendance so your team understands the reporting impact.
