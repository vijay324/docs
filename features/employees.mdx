## What it does

Employees module manages team profiles, invites, role assignments, and lifecycle updates.

## Why it matters

- Keeps organization structure current.
- Enables permission-safe access to features.
- Improves collaboration with accurate team data.

## How to use it effectively

1. Invite users with the right role from day one.
2. Keep department and title information current.
3. Audit inactive or outdated accounts regularly.
4. Use employee views to support planning and resourcing.
