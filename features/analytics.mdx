## What it does

Analytics shows productivity, completion trends, team performance, and delivery health.

## Why it matters

- Replaces guesswork with measurable insights.
- Helps managers identify bottlenecks quickly.
- Supports better planning for future sprints and workload.

## How to use it effectively

1. Review analytics weekly with leads.
2. Compare planned work vs completed work.
3. Watch overdue and cycle-time trends.
4. Use insights to rebalance ownership and priorities.

## Leadership rhythm

- Weekly: team-level performance review.
- Sprint-end: delivery outcome review.
- Monthly: cross-team capacity and trend analysis.
