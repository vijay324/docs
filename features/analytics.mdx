Analytics adapts to your role, data access, and plan. Use the scope and Project filters to focus the results you can access.

## Analytics workspace

| Tab | What you can analyze |
| --- | --- |
| Overview | Delivery health, risks, completed work, trends, executive brief, anomalies, and insights |
| Work | Projects, Sprints, Tasks, flow reliability, quality, blockers, and deadlines |
| People | Team delivery, workload, attendance, leave, and departments |
| Compare | Scopes, periods, metrics, and leaderboards available to your plan |

**Work** includes Projects, Sprints, Tasks, and Deadlines. **People** includes Team, Workload, Attendance, Leave, and Departments. Attendance is organization-scoped and does not inherit a Project filter.

Some metrics need enough history to calculate a useful result. Flotick shows an empty or unavailable state when the underlying data is insufficient.

## Save, export, and schedule

Save the current filters as a private or organization view and optionally make it your default. Exports reflect the selected scope, period, and filters.

On Max, schedule a saved view as a weekly or monthly report. See [Scheduled reports](/features/scheduled-reports).

## Interpret metrics responsibly

Analytics reflects work recorded in Flotick. Review task flow, estimates, dates, leave, attendance, and delivery context together. A metric does not by itself measure individual effort, performance, or quality.
