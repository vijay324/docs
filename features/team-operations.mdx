title: "Team Operations"
description: "Attendance, leave, payroll, employee management, and organizational policy controls."
---

## Attendance

- Check-in/check-out workflows in `/{orgSlug}/attendance`.
- Live work timer and daily attendance views.
- Team overview and attendance analytics for managers/admins.
- AI-assisted attendance description enhancement endpoint.

## Leave management

- Balance tracking and leave requests in `/{orgSlug}/leave`.
- Approval/rejection workflows for managers and admins.
- Upcoming and pending leave pipelines for planning.

## Payroll

- Payroll center in `/{orgSlug}/payroll`.
- Salary structures, compensation, payroll runs, and payslips.
- Tabs for operational segmentation (overview, runs, structures, slips).

## Employee lifecycle

- Employee directory in `/{orgSlug}/employees`.
- Invite flows, profile updates, and role management.
- Onboarding status and account controls.

## Policies

- Attendance and leave policy configuration in `/{orgSlug}/settings/policies`.
- Reset-to-default support for controlled updates.

<Tip>
  Operations data can be correlated with delivery data in analytics to quantify team capacity and output trends together.
</Tip>
