## Record your day

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check in">
    Open **Attendance**, click **Check in**, and confirm your work type. Flotick creates one attendance record for the current date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out">
    Click **Check out** and add a work description. The app calculates the recorded duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Use the table or calendar view to review your history. Statuses include `Checked In`, `Checked Out`, `Pending`, and `Leave`.

## Missed or incorrect attendance

Use **Forgot attendance** to submit a past entry when policy allows. For corrections, create a regularization request with the date, reason, requested times, work type, and description. You can review your requests and cancel an eligible pending request.

Admins can review organization requests. Approved regularization creates or updates the underlying attendance record.

## Team view

The paid-plan team view shows organization attendance for authorized leaders. Past-date edits and team actions remain subject to role and attendance policy.

AI Enhance can rewrite a work description before you save it. See [AI Enhance](/features/ai-enhance).

## Understand the record

Each attendance record is tied to one member and one calendar date. It can include check-in and check-out time, work type, work description, status, and recorded duration. A check-in starts the day; checking out records the end of the day rather than creating a second record.

Your organization's attendance policy determines working days, past-date allowance, grace periods, and hour thresholds. A member shift can provide more specific timings and thresholds for that member. See [Attendance policy](/admin/attendance-policy) and [Shifts](/features/shifts).

## Regularize an entry

Use a regularization request when the date already has an incorrect record or when you need to explain a missed punch. Choose the correct request type, provide a reason, and enter requested check-in/out times or corrected work information. The request remains `Pending` until an authorized reviewer approves or rejects it. Approval updates the corresponding attendance record; rejection preserves the original record.

## Team and request views

Authorized leaders can switch to the team view to inspect organization attendance by date. The regularization section separates **My Requests** from **Team Requests**, making it clear which changes need a decision. Use filters and date controls before reviewing a large team.

<Tip>
Use a concise check-out description that names the outcome, not just the activity. This gives Analytics and Orbit better context without requiring a separate daily report.
</Tip>
