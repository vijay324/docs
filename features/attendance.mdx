Flotick attendance is built around a simple promise: **one record per member per day**. Members check in, check out, and add a brief description of what they worked on. Managers see a live picture of their team.

[IMAGE: Attendance page with table, calendar, and team overview tabs]

## Check in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the check-in dialog">
    Click **Check in** in the sidebar or top bar. The dialog appears anywhere in the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick your work type">
    `Office`, `Remote`, or `Hybrid`. Your default work type comes from your profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the time">
    Flotick suggests now. Adjust if needed (admins control whether past-date entries are allowed).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit">
    Your day record is created with status `Checked In`. The **live work timer** starts ticking in the top bar.

    [IMAGE: Check-in dialog with work type selector]
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Check out

When you're done for the day, click **Check out**.

- Add a brief **work description** in plain text or Markdown
- Click **Enhance with AI** to polish the description into a clean recap (see [AI Enhance](/features/ai-enhance))
- Confirm the time

Your day record updates to `Checked Out` with the duration calculated automatically. **Cross-midnight check-outs are supported** — the duration accounts for the day boundary.

[IMAGE: Check-out dialog with work description and Enhance with AI button]

## Three views

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Table">
    Your full attendance log. Filter by month, status, or work type.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Calendar">
    A color-coded calendar:
    - 🟢 **Checked Out** — completed day
    - 🟡 **Checked In** — in progress
    - ⚪ **Pending** — future or unrecorded
    - 🔵 **Leave** — approved leave day
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Team overview">
    Managers and Admins see who's checked in, on leave, or absent. Aggregated stats by date range help with capacity planning.

    [IMAGE: Team attendance overview with member status grid]
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Late arrival and overtime

Both rules are configurable in the [attendance policy](/admin/attendance-policy):

- **Late arrival window** — flag check-ins after a threshold (e.g., 10:00)
- **Overtime** — durations longer than 8 hours are flagged automatically and feed payroll OT calculation (1.5×)

## Forgotten check-out

If a member forgets to check out, an Admin or Manager can backfill from the **Forgotten attendance** dialog. The action is recorded with the admin as the creator for audit.

## Leave and attendance

When a leave request is approved, Flotick automatically creates or updates the matching attendance rows with status `Leave`. You won't need to back-mark anything — the systems stay in sync.

## Live team picture

The team overview updates in real time as your colleagues check in or out. Useful for stand-ups and same-day capacity questions.

## Tips

- Encourage a daily check-out habit — work descriptions become a great input for the AI weekly summary.
- Use **work type** consistently to power hybrid policy reporting.
- If your team doesn't track attendance, ask your Admin to disable it from organization settings — Flotick's other features still work without it.
