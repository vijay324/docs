title: "Attendance"
description: "Track daily work presence and time accountability with minimal effort."
---

## What it does

Attendance captures check-in/check-out times, work type, and daily work logs.

## Why it matters

- Improves transparency across teams.
- Supports payroll readiness and compliance.
- Creates reliable records for performance discussions.

## How to use it effectively

1. Check in at start of day.
2. Select correct work type (office, remote, hybrid).
3. Check out with a short summary of completed work.
4. Review attendance history weekly for consistency.
