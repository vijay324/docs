## Record your day

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check in">
    Open **Attendance**, click **Check in**, and confirm your work type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check out">
    Click **Check out**, add a work description, and save. Flotick calculates the recorded duration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Use **Table** or **Calendar** view to review your history. A record can include check-in and check-out time, work type, description, status, and duration.

[AI Enhance](/features/ai-enhance) can rewrite the work description before you save it.

## Missed or incorrect attendance

Use **Forgot attendance** for a past entry when the organization policy allows it. Use **Regularization** to request a correction with the date, reason, requested times, work type, and description.

A request stays `Pending` until an authorized reviewer approves or rejects it. Approval creates or updates the attendance record. You can review your own requests and cancel an eligible pending request.

## Team attendance

On eligible plans, authorized leaders can use **Team Overview**, filter by member or department, and review team regularization requests. **My Requests** and **Team Requests** keep personal corrections separate from decisions awaiting review.

The [attendance policy](/admin/attendance-policy) controls standard hours, overtime threshold, default work type, weekend requirements, and whether backdated entries are allowed.
