## What it does

Projects are the main container for team goals, tasks, and sprint execution.

## Why it matters

- Keeps related work grouped and easy to manage.
- Improves ownership clarity across teams.
- Makes reporting and planning more reliable.

## How to use it effectively

1. Create one project per initiative or product area.
2. Assign clear owners and contributors.
3. Set timeline expectations before tasks are created.
4. Use project views to track progress and risks.

## Best practices

- Avoid oversized projects with unrelated outcomes.
- Review project health weekly with leads.
