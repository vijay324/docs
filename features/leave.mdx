---
title: "Leave Management"
description: "Handle leave requests and approvals with clarity and fairness."
---

## What it does

Leave Management lets employees request leave and enables managers/admins to approve or reject quickly.

## Why it matters

- Prevents scheduling surprises.
- Makes approval decisions transparent.
- Keeps leave balances accurate.

## How to use it effectively

1. Submit leave as early as possible.
2. Add clear date range and leave type.
3. Managers review pending requests daily.
4. Use upcoming leave views for planning team coverage.
