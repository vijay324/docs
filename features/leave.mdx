## Request leave

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Leave">
    Click **New request**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the request">
    Choose a leave type, start and end dates, optional half-day period, and reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    The request starts as `Pending`. You can cancel it while policy and status allow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Supported leave codes are `PL`, `SL`, `CL`, `EL`, `ML`, `PTL`, `CO`, and `LOP`. Your organization controls which are enabled and how each name is presented.

## Review leave

Admins and Managers with leave-edit access can open pending or team requests, then approve or reject with an optional review comment. Approved leave updates balances and links applicable attendance dates.

## Balances and advanced workflows

The page shows available balance and request history. Paid-plan leave management can also include:

- Comp-off requests and decisions
- Balance accrual and carry-forward runs
- Balance adjustments and transaction history
- Leave encashment settings

Holidays, weekends, overlap rules, notice periods, and half-day behavior follow the organization's [leave policy](/admin/leave-policy).

## Review your request history

The **My requests** tab separates pending, approved, rejected, and cancelled leave. Open a request to review its dates, day count, type, reason, reviewer, and any review comment. Cancel a pending request when the action is available; approved leave may require an Admin or Manager to resolve it according to organization policy.

## Approve or reject requests

Authorized reviewers use the **Pending** or **Team** tabs. Review the member, leave type, dates, requested day count, available balance, and overlap information before making a decision. Add a short comment so the decision is clear to the member. Approval updates the request state and applicable balance; rejection leaves the request recorded with the decision.

## Comp-off and accrual

On eligible plans, members can request compensatory time off for approved work on a holiday or weekend. Authorized reviewers can approve, reject, expire, or mark comp-off as used. Admins can run configured leave accrual and year-end carry-forward workflows; use these controls only after reviewing policy settings and the intended financial period.

## Leave types and balances

Leave codes are organization-configurable. A member's balance tracks opening amount, accrued, used, adjustments, carried forward, encashed, and lapsed days. The balance is a record of approved transactions, not a simple editable total—use the appropriate administrative adjustment flow when a correction is needed.
