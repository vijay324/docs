## Request leave

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create the request">
    Click **New request**, then choose an enabled leave type and dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set the duration">
    Choose a full day or an available half-day period and add the reason.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review the calculated day count and submit. The request starts as `Pending`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your organization's policy determines enabled types, available balance, past-date behavior, notice, maximum consecutive days, and whether weekends count.

## Leave workspace

| Tab | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| My requests | Review your request history and decisions |
| Pending | Approve or reject requests when authorized |
| Team | Review organization leave when authorized |
| Upcoming | See approved upcoming absences |
| Comp-off | Claim and review compensatory time off |
| Accrual | Run and inspect balance workflows when authorized |

Open a request to see its dates, day count, type, reason, reviewer, and review comment. Cancel an eligible pending request from its available actions.

## Approvals and balances

Authorized reviewers can approve or reject a request and add a comment. Approval updates the applicable leave balance and attendance dates.

On eligible plans, leave management also supports comp-off claims and decisions, accrual runs, carry-forward, balance adjustments and history, and encashment workflows. Balance history separates opening, accrued, used, adjusted, carried, encashed, and lapsed amounts.

See [Leave policy](/admin/leave-policy) for the organization controls applied to new requests and future accrual.
