---
title: "AI Summaries"
description: "Generate narrative briefs from current organization metrics."
---

AI Summaries turn Flotick metrics into a written brief. They appear in supported analytics surfaces and as report templates in Orbit.

Available templates include weekly and monthly summaries, Sprint summaries, bottleneck detection, deadline risk, workload balance, team health, leadership digest, founder snapshot, HR delivery review, and performance-oriented summaries.

## Generate a summary

1. Open the supported analytics brief or [Orbit](/features/orbit).
2. Choose a template and scope when offered.
3. Generate the report.
4. Review the source metrics and submit feedback on the result.

Generated summaries and usage are recorded so supported history views can retrieve previous reports. Generation consumes the organization's monthly AI allowance.

<Note>
AI-generated text is a decision aid. Use the underlying Task, Sprint, attendance, or leave records as the authoritative source.
</Note>

## Choose the right summary

| Need | Useful template |
| --- | --- |
| Weekly delivery update | Weekly summary or leadership digest |
| Sprint review | Sprint summary, bottleneck detection, or deadline risk |
| Capacity conversation | Workload balance or team health |
| Leadership update | Founder snapshot or leadership digest |
| People and delivery review | HR delivery review |

Templates use the selected or inferred reporting context. A result is most useful when the related Tasks have current statuses, estimates, points, and due dates, and when attendance and leave records are complete.

## Review a generated brief

Read the summary for trends, risks, and actions, then verify the numbers in Analytics and the relevant work records. Use the feedback control to indicate whether a result was useful. Feedback helps Flotick capture quality signals for later review; it does not automatically change the source data.

## Usage behavior

AI summary requests count against organization AI allowance. A generation can be unavailable when no eligible data exists for the requested scope, when the feature is not included in the plan, or when the monthly allowance is exhausted.
