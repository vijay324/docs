AI Summaries turn your Flotick data into clean, useful narrative reports in seconds. Pick a template and a scope; Flotick collects the right context and streams a summary back in real time.

[IMAGE: AI summary widget on the dashboard with template picker and Generate button]

## What you can generate

| Template | Best for |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Weekly summary** | A team-wide recap of last week. |
| **Monthly summary** | Higher-altitude view spanning a month. |
| **Sprint summary** | Recap of a single sprint, including velocity and outcomes. |
| **Bottleneck detection** | Find where work is piling up. |
| **Top and bottom performers** | A read on contribution patterns. |
| **Workload balance** | Spotting imbalances across the team. |
| **Deadline risk** | Highlight upcoming due dates at risk. |
| **Team health** | Composite signal across velocity, attendance, and reviews. |
| **Founder snapshot** | A founder-grade pulse on the org. |
| **Leadership digest** | Summary tuned for leadership readers. |
| **Custom** | Bring your own prompt. |

## Generate a summary

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the AI panel">
    Click the **AI summary** widget on your [Personal Dashboard](/features/personal-dashboard) or open it from **Analytics**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a template and scope">
    Each template tells you what scope it needs:

    - Org-wide: weekly, monthly, leadership digest
    - Project: monthly, bottlenecks
    - Sprint: sprint summary, retrospective help
    - Member: workload balance for a single person
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate">
    Click **Generate**. The summary streams in token-by-token (a typewriter effect). When it finishes, you can copy or share it.

    [IMAGE: AI summary streaming output in a card]
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Where context comes from

Flotick builds context from the right data automatically:

- Tasks, statuses, and points
- Sprint metrics and burndowns
- Attendance and leave activity
- Comments and recent updates

Encrypted long-form fields are not included in context.

## History and feedback

The **History** panel lists past summaries with their template and scope. Open any past summary to view it again.

Click 👍 or 👎 on a summary to leave feedback. We use the signal to improve prompts and templates.

## Custom prompts

The **Custom** template lets you bring your own prompt. Useful for "summarize what changed about Project X this week" or similar bespoke questions. Custom prompts may produce noisier output than the curated templates.

## Plan limits

AI usage is metered. Free plan organizations get a baseline; Pro and Max raise the limits. See [Plans](/admin/billing/plans).

## Tips

- Lead with a curated template before reaching for **Custom**.
- Generate the **Weekly summary** every Friday — your team will love the artifact.
- Pair AI summaries with [AI Insights](/features/ai-insights) for proactive alerts in your Inbox.
