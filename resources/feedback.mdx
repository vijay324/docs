---
title: "Send feedback"
description: "Share a rating and short message with the Flotick product team."
---

Open the profile menu in the app sidebar and choose **Feedback**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a topic">
    Select bug report, feature request, improvement, or other.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your feedback">
    Enter at least 10 characters. Include the page and expected behavior when reporting a problem.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a rating">
    Choose a rating from very poor to excellent, then click **Send**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Feedback is sent privately to the Flotick team. It does not create a trackable request in your organization. Use [Feature requests](/resources/feature-requests) when you want to follow a product request's status.

## Choose feedback or a feature request

Use **Feedback** for a quick product impression, an issue you do not need to track, or a short suggestion. Use **Feature Requests** when the idea or bug needs attachments, a more complete description, and a visible lifecycle. Use [Support](/resources/support) for account, billing, access, or security problems that need a response.

## Protect sensitive information

Feedback is not an appropriate place for passwords, verification codes, environment values, private keys, or confidential client data. Describe the scenario without reproducing the sensitive value.
