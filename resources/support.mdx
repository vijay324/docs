---
title: "Support"
description: "Get help with product, account, billing, or security issues."
---

Use the **Help** item in Flotick or email [support@flotick.com](mailto:support@flotick.com).

The in-app support menu prepares email templates for common categories so the right context is included.

## Include with your request

- Organization name and slug
- Page where the issue occurred
- Approximate date and time, including timezone
- What you expected and what happened
- Screenshot or screen recording without passwords, secrets, or personal data
- Browser and device when the issue is visual or sign-in related

For a product idea or non-urgent bug report you want to track, use [Feature requests](/resources/feature-requests).

For security concerns, put **Security** in the email subject. Do not send passwords, verification codes, environment values, or private keys.

## Choose the right route

| Need | Best route |
| --- | --- |
| How to use a feature | Search this documentation. |
| Product idea or trackable bug | [Feature requests](/resources/feature-requests) |
| Quick product impression | Use **Send feedback** in the app. |
| Sign-in, access, billing, or unexpected app behavior | In-app Help or support email |
| Security concern | Support email with **Security** in the subject |

## Help your Admin help you

Many access issues are resolved inside the organization. Ask an Admin to check your role, member activation state, Project membership, per-member permissions, plan status, and whether the relevant module is available. Include the exact page and the action you expected to see.
