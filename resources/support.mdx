We respond fast. Pick the channel that matches your need.

## Email

- **General support** — [support@flotick.com](mailto:support@flotick.com)
- **Security issues** — [security@flotick.com](mailto:security@flotick.com)
- **Sales and demos** — [sales@flotick.com](mailto:sales@flotick.com)

## In-app support

- **Help center** — open **Help** in the dashboard sidebar for guides and step-by-steps
- **Submit feedback** — click the feedback button (footer or help menu) to send a bug, idea, or comment directly to us

When sending a support request, please include:

- Your **organization slug** (the part of the URL after `app.flotick.com/`)
- Your **email** or member ID
- A short description and, if possible, a screenshot or screen recording
- The browser and operating system you're using

## Response times

- **Free** — best-effort, typically within two business days
- **Pro** — priority response within one business day
- **Max** — premium support with same-day response and onboarding assistance

## Status

For known incidents and infrastructure status, see the linked status page in the dashboard footer (or contact support).

## Roadmap input

Pro and Max customers have direct roadmap input. Email [sales@flotick.com](mailto:sales@flotick.com) to schedule a quarterly roadmap review.

## Community

We're keeping the community small and high-signal. Watch [Flotick on LinkedIn](https://linkedin.com/company/flotick) for product updates, and follow [@flotick on X](https://x.com/flotick).
