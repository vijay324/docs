## Notes

- Features and limits change as Flotick evolves. The pricing page is the source of truth for current limits.
- Some advanced AI templates are gated specifically by AI usage caps, not by feature toggles. Heavy users may need a higher plan.
- All security capabilities (2FA, trusted devices, encryption at rest, audit) are available on every plan.
