## Notes

- Features and limits evolve. The [pricing page](https://flotick.com/pricing) is authoritative for current limits.
- Some AI templates are gated by monthly **usage limits**, not feature toggles — heavy users may need a higher plan.
- Per-member overrides can fine-tune the role defaults above within each role's ceiling — see [Member permissions](/admin/member-permissions).
- Security capabilities (2FA, trusted devices, encryption at rest, audit) are available on every plan.
