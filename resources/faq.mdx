<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I see a page or action?">
    Availability depends on your role, plan, Project access, and any per-member override. Ask an Admin to review your member permissions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Where is my dashboard?">
    Flotick opens Inbox after sign-in. Inbox is your focused Task workspace. Use Analytics for metrics and My Board for a personal Project Kanban.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why is a Task missing?">
    Check Task scope, Project selector, filters, and **Show completed sprint tasks**. Deleted Tasks appear in Trash when you have access.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I recover deleted work?">
    Supported Tasks, Sprints, Projects, members, and files stay in Trash for 30 days. Permanent deletion cannot be reversed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I upload a file?">
    Check organization storage, the plan's per-file size, entity limits, and your file-create permission. Task uploads require Pro or Max.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can’t I create another Project or invite a member?">
    The organization may have reached its active-Project or seat limit. Archive work, free a seat, or change plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Orbit make changes automatically?">
    Orbit Chat is read-only. Orbit Agent shows Admins and Managers a plan and requires approval before running changes. It cannot delete records.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I contact Flotick?">
    Use the in-app Help menu or [Support](/resources/support). Include your organization slug and the time the issue occurred.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
