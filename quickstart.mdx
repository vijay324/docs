## Who this guide is for

- Workspace owners setting up Flotick for the first time.
- Team leads preparing project and sprint workflows.
- Managers establishing operational consistency.

## 1) Create your organization workspace

- Complete org setup and confirm your organization name and slug.
- Set timezone and working preferences so reports stay accurate.
- Add your first department(s) for cleaner assignment and reporting.

## 2) Invite your core team

- Invite admins and managers first.
- Add employees by team or department.
- Use role-appropriate access from day one.

<Tip>
  Start with a small pilot group (5-15 users), validate your workflows, then roll out to the full organization.
</Tip>

## 3) Set up your first project

- Create a project with clear ownership.
- Add goals, due dates, and key members.
- Decide whether the project is public or private.

## 4) Build your task workflow

- Create initial tasks and assign clear owners.
- Use priority and due dates to focus execution.
- Move work through statuses to keep everyone aligned.

## 5) Launch your first sprint

- Define sprint goal, timeline, and scope.
- Move selected tasks into the sprint.
- Use board and analytics views for daily visibility.

## 6) Activate daily operations

- Encourage daily check-in/out in Attendance.
- Enable leave request and approval flow.
- Review dashboards daily to identify blockers early.
